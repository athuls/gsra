Enumerations

MaterialType

Supported material types.

  • ELASTIC Elastic material (default).
  • ORTHOTROPIC Orthotropic material.
  • NONLINEARELASTIC Nonlinear-elastic material.
  • PLASTICBILINEAR Plastic bilinear material.
  • PLASTICMULTILINEAR Plastic muli-linear material.
  • THERMOISOTROPIC Thermo isotropic material.
  • THERMOORTHOTROPIC Thermo orthotropic material.

Unit

Supported units.

  • METER Meter (SI) (default).
  • KILOGRAM Kilogram (SI).
  • NEWTON Newton (SI).
  • FOOT Foot (1200/3937 meters; imperial).
  • POUND Pound (international avoirdupois pound; 0.45359237 kilogram).
  • KILOPOUND 1000 Pounds (imperial).
  • SLUG Mass that accelerates by 1 ft/s^2 (imperial)
  • KILOSLUG 1000 Slugs (imperial).

Quantity

Available quantities (degrees of freedom).

  • XDISPLACEMENT X displacement (default).
  • YDISPLACEMENT Y displacement.
  • ZDISPLACEMENT Z displacement.
  • XROTATION X rotation
  • YROTATION Y rotation
  • ZROTATION Z rotation
  • XFORCE X force
  • YFORCE Y force
  • ZFORCE Z force
  • RAW Raw (undefined).

CoordinateSystemType

Supported coordinate system types.

  • CARTESIAN Cartesian coordinate system (default)
  • SPHERICAL Spherical coordinate system
  • CYLINDRICAL Cylindrical coordinate system

BoundaryType

Supported boundary types.

  • FREE Free to move (default).
  • FIXED Fixed.

SectionType

Supported cross sections.

  • BOX
  • PROPERTIES
  • RECTANGULAR
  • PIPE
  • I
  • L
  • U

GroupType

Supported group types.

  • BEAM
  • TRUSS
  • GENERAL
  • ISOBEAM
  • PLATE
  • SHELL
  • SPRING
  • THREEDSOLID
  • TWODSOLID

SensorType

Supported sensor types.

  • ACCELEROMETER Accelerometer, measuring linear acceleration.
  • ANEMOMETER Anemometer, measuring wind speed.
  • WINDVANE Wind vane, measuring wind direction.
  • STRAINGAUGE Strain gauge.
